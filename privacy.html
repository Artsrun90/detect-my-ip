Privacy Policy

How we protect your privacy and handle your data

Information We Collect

DetectMyIP is designed with privacy in mind. We only collect the minimum information necessary to provide our services:

  • IP Address: We detect and display your public IP address for informational purposes
  • Location Data: We may determine your approximate location based on your IP address
  • Browser Information: Basic browser data to ensure compatibility

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:

  • Display your IP address and network information
  • Provide geolocation services
  • Improve our service functionality
  • Ensure proper website operation

Data Storage and Retention

We do not store your personal information on our servers. All data processing is done in real-time and is not retained after the session ends. Your IP address and location data are not logged or stored permanently.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services to provide IP detection and geolocation functionality. These services may have their own privacy policies. We use reputable providers that prioritize user privacy.

Cookies and Tracking

DetectMyIP does not use cookies for tracking purposes. We may use essential cookies for website functionality, but these do not contain personal information.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect any information we process. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Know what information we collect about you
  • Request access to your information
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Object to processing of your information

Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our website.

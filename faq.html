Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about DetectMyIP service

What is DetectMyIP?

DetectMyIP is a free online service that helps you discover your public IP address, get detailed geolocation information, and test your internet connection speed. We provide accurate, real-time information about your internet connection.

How do I check my IP address?

Simply visit our homepage and your IP address will be automatically detected and displayed. No registration or installation required - just open the page and see your IP information instantly.

Is my personal information safe?

Yes, absolutely. We only collect the minimum necessary information to provide our service. We don't store personal data, track your browsing habits, or share your information with third parties. All data is processed in real-time and not saved on our servers.

Why does my IP address differ from my real location?

Your IP address is assigned by your Internet Service Provider (ISP) and may not always reflect your exact physical location. This can happen due to ISP routing, proxy servers, or VPN usage. Our geolocation data shows where your ISP is located, not necessarily where you are.

How accurate is the speed test?

Our speed test provides a good estimate of your internet speed, but results can vary based on your connection, server load, and other factors. For the most accurate results, close other applications and ensure you're not downloading anything else during the test.

Can I use this service with a VPN?

Yes, you can use our service with a VPN. In fact, our service can help you verify that your VPN is working correctly by showing the IP address and location that your VPN is using.

What's the difference between IPv4 and IPv6?

IPv4 and IPv6 are different versions of the Internet Protocol. IPv4 uses 32-bit addresses (like 192.168.1.1), while IPv6 uses 128-bit addresses (like 2001:0db8:85a3::8a2e:0370:7334). IPv6 provides more address space and is the future of internet addressing.

Why is my ping so high?

High ping can be caused by several factors: distance to the server, network congestion, ISP routing issues, or using a VPN. A ping under 50ms is excellent, 50-100ms is good, and over 100ms may indicate connection issues.

Is this service free?

Yes, DetectMyIP is completely free to use. There are no hidden fees, subscriptions, or premium features. You can use all our tools as many times as you want without any cost.

Can I use this service on mobile devices?

Absolutely! Our service is fully responsive and works perfectly on smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers. You can check your IP address and test your internet speed from any device with a web browser.

Getting Started

Welcome to DetectMyIP! This guide will help you make the most of our free IP detection and speed testing service.

What You'll Need

  • A device with internet connection (computer, smartphone, or tablet)
  • A modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge)
  • No registration or installation required!

Supported Browsers

  • Google Chrome (recommended)
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Safari
  • Microsoft Edge
  • Opera

IP Address Detection

Our service automatically detects your public IP address when you visit our homepage.

How It Works

  1. Visit our homepage
  2. Your IP address is automatically detected
  3. View your IPv4 and IPv6 addresses
  4. See detailed information about your connection

Understanding Your Results

IPv4 Address

Your primary internet address (e.g., 192.168.1.1). This is the most commonly used IP address format.

IPv6 Address

Your modern internet address (e.g., 2001:0db8:85a3::8a2e:0370:7334). This is the future of internet addressing.

What Information We Show

  • IP Address: Your public internet address
  • Location: City, region, and country
  • ISP: Your internet service provider
  • Timezone: Your local time zone
  • Security: Proxy and VPN detection

Internet Speed Testing

Test your internet connection speed with our built-in speed test tool.

How to Run a Speed Test

  1. Scroll down to the "Speed Test" section
  2. Click the "Start Test" button
  3. Wait for the test to complete (usually 10-30 seconds)
  4. View your results: ping, download speed, and overall quality

Preparing for Accurate Results

⚠️ Important Tips

  • Close other applications and browser tabs
  • Disconnect other devices from your network
  • Use a wired connection when possible
  • Test at different times of day

Understanding Speed Test Results

Metric What It Means Good Values
Ping Response time to server < 50ms
Download Speed How fast you receive data > 25 Mbps
Overall Quality Combined performance rating Good or Excellent

Geolocation Information

Our service provides detailed location information based on your IP address.

Location Accuracy

IP-based geolocation is generally accurate to the city level, but may not reflect your exact physical location due to:

  • ISP routing and infrastructure
  • Proxy server usage
  • VPN connections
  • Mobile network routing

What Location Data We Show

  • Country: Your country of connection
  • Region/State: Your state or province
  • City: Your city or nearest major city
  • Coordinates: Approximate latitude and longitude

Troubleshooting

Common issues and solutions when using DetectMyIP.

Speed Test Not Working

Problem:

Speed test fails or shows very low speeds

Solutions:

  • Check your internet connection
  • Close other applications
  • Try again in a few minutes
  • Contact your ISP if problems persist

IP Address Not Detected

Problem:

IP address shows as "Unknown" or "Not detected"

Solutions:

  • Refresh the page
  • Check your internet connection
  • Disable ad blockers temporarily
  • Try a different browser

Location Information Incorrect

Problem:

Location shows wrong city or country

Explanation:

This is normal! IP geolocation shows where your ISP is located, not necessarily where you are physically located.

Pro Tips

Get the most out of DetectMyIP with these expert tips.

For Accurate Speed Tests

  • Test at different times of day to see peak vs. off-peak performance
  • Use a wired connection for the most accurate results
  • Test multiple times and average the results
  • Compare results with your ISP's advertised speeds

For Privacy Testing

  • Test your IP before and after connecting to a VPN
  • Check if your proxy is working correctly
  • Verify your location is properly masked
  • Test from different networks (home, work, mobile)

For Network Troubleshooting

  • Use our service to verify your connection is working
  • Check if your ISP is providing the promised speeds
  • Identify if location-based restrictions are affecting you
  • Monitor your connection quality over time

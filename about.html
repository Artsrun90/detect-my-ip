About DetectMyIP

Your trusted source for IP address information and network tools

Who We Are

DetectMyIP is a comprehensive network diagnostic platform that provides instant access to your IP address information, geolocation data, and advanced network tools. Our service helps users understand their internet connection, troubleshoot network issues, and analyze their online presence with professional-grade accuracy.

Our Mission

We believe that network transparency should be accessible to everyone. Whether you're a developer debugging connectivity issues, a system administrator configuring servers, or simply curious about your internet connection, DetectMyIP provides the essential tools you need in a clean, intuitive interface that works on any device.

What We Offer

IP Address Detection

Instantly detect your public IPv4 and IPv6 addresses with real-time accuracy and detailed connection information

Geolocation Services

Get precise location data including city, country, timezone, and ISP information based on your IP address

Speed Testing

Test your internet connection speed with our advanced speedometer tool that measures download speed and ping latency

DNS Lookup

Check domain DNS records including A, AAAA, MX, CNAME, and other record types for any domain name

Proxy Detection

Detect if you're using a proxy or VPN service and identify potential security or privacy concerns

Blacklist Checker

Check if your IP address is listed in any major email or security blacklists that could affect your online reputation
